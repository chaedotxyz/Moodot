# Privacy Policy

**Moodot — Notes & Mood Tracker for Mac**

Last Updated Date: March 2026

Welcome to Moodot. We understand that your personal journal entries and emotional data are deeply private. This Privacy Policy explains how Moodot handles your information and affirms our commitment to keeping your data entirely in your hands.

## Information Collection

Moodot does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All content you create within the app — including your written notes, mood selections, and timestamps — remains exclusively on your device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

## Local Storage of Data

Every note, mood entry, and preference you set in Moodot is saved directly to your Mac's local storage. This includes your journal entries, mood states across the 8-point spectrum (melancholy through euphoric), and auto-saved timestamps.

## Timestamp and City Display

Each note is automatically timestamped using your Mac's system clock. The city name displayed alongside each entry is derived solely from your Mac's system timezone setting (for example, `Asia/Seoul` displays as "Seoul"). Moodot does not access your device's GPS, location services, or any network-based location data. No location information is collected or transmitted — the city name is read entirely from your local system preferences.

## No Data Sharing

Because Moodot does not collect any personal data, there is nothing to share. We do not sell, license, or disclose user information to third parties under any circumstances. Your thoughts and emotional records are yours alone.

## Data Security

The security of your Moodot data is directly tied to the security of your Mac. We recommend enabling a strong login password or biometric authentication (such as Touch ID) to protect access to your device. Moodot does not offer cloud backup or data recovery services, so we encourage you to maintain your own backups if needed.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable regulations. Any updates will be posted at this location with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
